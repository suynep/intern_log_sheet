### Technical "Today I Learned" (TIL)

#### Classes are blueprints, not containers. 
Properly defining responsibility inside a class (instead of dumping logic into it) significantly improves maintainability and readability.

#### Constructors shape object integrity. 
Using named constructors, initializer lists, and const constructors ensures objects are created in valid states from the start rather than patched afterward.

#### Effective Dart is about clarity over cleverness. 
Naming conventions, immutability where possible, and limiting public surface area are not stylistic preferences; they are scalability decisions.

#### Inheritance (extends) should model “is-a” relationships only. 
Misusing inheritance for code reuse leads to tight coupling; composition is often safer.

#### Mixins enable behavior reuse without hierarchy pollution. 
They provide modular capability injection while avoiding deep inheritance chains.

#### OOP is about modeling reality precisely. 
The quality of abstraction determines how naturally future features fit into the system.
